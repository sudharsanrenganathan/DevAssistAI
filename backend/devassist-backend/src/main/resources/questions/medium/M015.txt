ID: M015
TITLE: Matrix Transpose
DIFFICULTY: MEDIUM

DESCRIPTION:
Given a matrix, print its transpose.

INPUT FORMAT:
- First line contains rows R and columns C
- Next R lines contain C integers each

OUTPUT FORMAT:
- Print transpose of the matrix

SAMPLE TEST CASES:
INPUT:
2 3
1 2 3
4 5 6
OUTPUT:
1 4
2 5
3 6
EXPLANATION:
Rows become columns.

INPUT:
1 2
7 8
OUTPUT:
7
8
EXPLANATION:
Transpose of 1×2 matrix.

INPUT:
2 1
5
6
OUTPUT:
5 6
EXPLANATION:
Transpose of 2×1 matrix.

HIDDEN TEST CASES:
INPUT:
3 3
1 2 3
4 5 6
7 8 9
OUTPUT:
1 4 7
2 5 8
3 6 9

INPUT:
1 1
10
OUTPUT:
10

CONSTRAINTS:
- 1 ≤ R, C ≤ 100
