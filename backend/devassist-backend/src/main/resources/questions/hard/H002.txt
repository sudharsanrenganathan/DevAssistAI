ID: H002
TITLE: Merge Intervals
DIFFICULTY: HARD

DESCRIPTION:
Given a list of intervals, merge all overlapping intervals.

INPUT FORMAT:
- First line contains integer N
- Next N lines contain two integers start and end

OUTPUT FORMAT:
- Print merged intervals in increasing order

SAMPLE TEST CASES:
INPUT:
4
1 3
2 6
8 10
15 18
OUTPUT:
1 6
8 10
15 18
EXPLANATION:
Overlapping intervals are merged.

INPUT:
1
1 4
OUTPUT:
1 4
EXPLANATION:
Single interval.

INPUT:
2
1 4
4 5
OUTPUT:
1 5
EXPLANATION:
Touching intervals are merged.

HIDDEN TEST CASES:
INPUT:
3
1 5
2 3
4 6
OUTPUT:
1 6

INPUT:
2
5 7
1 3
OUTPUT:
1 3
5 7

CONSTRAINTS:
- 1 ≤ N ≤ 10^5
