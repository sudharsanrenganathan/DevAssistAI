ID: H015
TITLE: Maximum Rectangle in Histogram
DIFFICULTY: HARD

DESCRIPTION:
Given heights of histogram bars, find the area of the largest rectangle.

INPUT FORMAT:
- First line contains N
- Second line contains N integers

OUTPUT FORMAT:
- Print maximum rectangle area

SAMPLE TEST CASES:
INPUT:
6
2 1 5 6 2 3
OUTPUT:
10
EXPLANATION:
Largest rectangle area is 10.

INPUT:
1
5
OUTPUT:
5
EXPLANATION:
Single bar.

INPUT:
4
2 4 2 1
OUTPUT:
6
EXPLANATION:
Rectangle covering bars [2,4,2].

HIDDEN TEST CASES:
INPUT:
5
1 2 3 4 5
OUTPUT:
9

INPUT:
5
5 4 3 2 1
OUTPUT:
9

CONSTRAINTS:
- 1 ≤ N ≤ 10^5
